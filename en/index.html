Jorge Graça (aka. fauxclore)

faux.clo.re | "Folclore" male name

  1. Artistic project that seeks to explore the intersections between the world of electronic music and ethnomusicological excavations of Portuguese folklore;
  2. Epithet of the artist developing this project;
  3. Musician, researcher, inventor of sound objects;

Research

Academic articles and publications, and other scientific participations.

CyberBird 1.0

Sound object that transforms human song into bird song.

CyberBird 3.0

Android application that changes your "bird song" by moving your mobile phone.

Canta Ceifeira

Album released in 2022 that combines saxophone, electronic music and traditional Portuguese music

Postcards

Community Music project developed in 2022 with a community from the Fátima Rehabilitation and Integration Centre and Ourearte students.

Camila

Music box that responds to the presence of people.

Alfabetário

Software that transforms what you type on a keyboard into typographic paintings and soundscapes.

CyberBoard

An ironing board that emulates a continuous bass, where moving the iron is like playing the strings of a harp.

Simurgh

Synthesiser and sampler incorporated into a bird mask, in which changing its expressions modifies the sound content produced.

Ornitopiano

Automatic piano that both imitates birdsong and emits low notes.

Imaginary Birds

Interactive workshop focussing on and exploring birdsong, from the real to the digital and the imaginary..

Anti-Censório

Typewriter that works as a synthesiser when you write on it.

Relator

Interactive phones that tell a series of stories when the handset is lifted.
