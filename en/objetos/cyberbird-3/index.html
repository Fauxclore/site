CyberBird 3.0

Like CyberBird 1.0, CyberBird 3.0 is a kind of digital bird created by man to simulate interactions with real birds. However, this is a more restless bird that challenges us to move around, involving us in a kind of joint dance.

CyberBird 3.0 consists of a mobile application for Android that, through buttons and abstract instructions, leads us on an exploration of the interactions between humans, birds and technology.

Developed in 2021, in conjunction with the Companhia de Música Teatral, which owns the rights to the image used in this article.

To access Cyberbird 3.0 you must:

  1. Download the application
  2. Install the file from the folder you downloaded it to
  3. Press Play, choose one of the birds, move the mobile phone (CyberBird 3.0 reacts to movement)
  4. Follow the suggestions/rules that the application sends you
