Alfabetário

Alfabetário is software developed in MaxMSP that transforms text typed on a computer keyboard into projected typographic paintings and soundscapes created from recordings of the alphabet in various languages and intonations.

In addition to allowing the creation of typographic compositions, such as the image above, Alfabetário algorithmically generates a poem at the end of its use, incorporating all the words entered in previous interactions. An example can be found below.

In workshops, Alfabetário promotes the literal and metaphorical deconstruction of words, exploring their semantic content and musicality.

Developed in 2022.
