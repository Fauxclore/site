Orquestra Coreográfica do Selvagem

In 2025, I had the pleasure and privilege of collaborating with Ordem do Ó in developing sound design for their ballets. The one referred to in this article was called Orquestra Coreográfica do Selvagem (Choreographic Orchestra of the Savage). Part of what made this ballet so interesting was the fact that it was a community project, involving amateur dancers alongside a team of professional dancers. For this ballet, I sought to base most of my composition on field recordings made during rehearsals. Ideally, I would not have used any sounds other than those recorded by me, but I had to make an exception for some timbres that I felt were missing. However, I feel that, in general, all the pieces share something ‘analogue’ between them, emerging from being constructed on the basis of sounds emitted by the same people, recorded in the same space, the amazing clearing in the Monsanto National Forest.
